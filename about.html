Jordan Hilliard

Environmental Design • 3D Design • Illustration

Based in Vancouver, I am a designer driven by the intersection of digital precision and organic expression. With a background spanning Environmental Design and 3D Art, I focus on creating immersive visual narratives that bridge the gap between architectural space and digital abstraction.

My practice is a constant exploration of tools. Whether I am manipulating 3D environments with KitBash3D or experimenting with old-school analog techniques, my goal remains the same: to find new ways of seeing using technology as my canvas while respecting the tactile roots of traditional art.

I’ve previously worked at the center of the design community, managing digital spaces and beta-testing unified 3D platforms. Today, I leverage that technical insight to help clients bring complex projects to life.
