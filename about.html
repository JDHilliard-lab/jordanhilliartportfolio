Hi, I'm Jordan.

Environment & 3D Designer

I am a multidisciplinary creative driven by design, strategy, and purposeful execution. With over two decades of experience spanning both the physical and digital realms, I specialize in creating site-specific art, bespoke illustrations, and 3D sculptures.

Rooted in curiosity and innovation, my work explores how art evolves through technology, transforming ideas into new forms of expression for hospitality, corporate, and healthcare spaces.

Whether it's shaping the visual narrative for the Ford archives or developing immersive architectural visualizations, my goal is always to bridge the gap between compelling storytelling and striking visual design.
